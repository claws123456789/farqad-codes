Christmas is celebrated on 25 December. Christians and non-Christians celebrated this festival with joy and happiness. In Christianity, Christmas is celebrated as the birth of Jesus Christ. Christmas is the time to spend time with family and friends and make memories. People also view Christmas as the end of the past and look forward towards the future. It is believed that Santa Claus visits everyone and distributes gifts. During Christmas, everyone sings carols to spread happiness and joy. People visit churches, read the bible and pray to God. Plays are also conducted at schools, churches, and other institutions depicting the birth of Jesus and the story of Mother Mary and Joseph. In many places, it snows during Christmas, and the snow makes the entire place like a magical winter wonderland.
